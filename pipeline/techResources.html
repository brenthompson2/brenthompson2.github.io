Awesome Inc U
Lexington, KY
awesomeincu.com

Coding school designed to help people learn how to create software (websites, mobile apps, or video games) through hands-on, in-person classes, online classes, office hours memberships, and on-site training.

Code Louisville
Louisville, KY
codelouisville.org

Collaboration of employers who want Louisville to be a place of technological innovation; offers free 12-week coding courses & resources to connect developers to employers.
