Ocean
Cincinnati, OH
oceanaccelerator.com

Faith-based accelerator; 5-month journey that builds infrastructure, deepens character, attracts investors.

UpTech
Northern Kentucky
uptechideas.org

Accepts up to 10 startups per year to participate in a 6 month program with up to $50,000 in funding for each team.

XLerate Health
Louisville, KY
xleratehealth.com

13-week training course that connects startups to mentors and leading health and wellness companies and offers a $20,000 stipend for 6% equity stake.

Awesome Fellowship
Lexington, KY
fellowship.awesomeinc.org

Mentor-driven program designed to accelerate high tech startups; provides mentorship and a variety of tech talent for up to 1 year.

The Brandery
Cincinnati, OH
brandery.org

Runs a 4 mo accelerator program for 10-12 companies for 6% equity stake in exchange for $50k, a year of free office space, and additional resources.
